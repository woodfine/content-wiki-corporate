HISTORY
AGENT: The Growth Strategy they are bringing to the market is not theoretical.
It is the direct evolution of Peter Woodfine’s 40-year track record as a developer, engineered to solve a specific, real-world financing short fall.
AGENT: Peter’s background starts from the ground up in construction, which naturally led to leasing in order to secure construction contracts.
This progressed into executing complete design-build projects for National Retailers.
He managed the entire lifecycle: site selection, permitting, supervision, and full construction services right through to turnover.
AGENT: Early on, he deliberately focused his operations on Regional Markets.
He identified that these regions possessed disposable income levels on par with Metro Markets, but there was an initial reluctance from National Retailers to enter them.
Over the last four decades, Peter has been highly successful in locating National Retailers throughout these Regional Markets.
AGENT: However, this success highlighted a persistent structural gap. Even with a National Retailer fully committed to a project, securing construction and take-out financing for real estate developments in Regional Markets remains fundamentally difficult due to systemic capital constraints.
The traditional commercial banking system simply does not adequately service the construction needs of these regions.
AGENT: To overcome this, Peter historically utilized a Joint Venture structure.
He required well-capitalized financial partners capable of providing a significantly larger percentage of equity than conventional financing dictates, along with the balance sheet capacity to guarantee conventional construction loans and take-out financing.
AGENT: While the legacy Joint Venture model was effective, it was not scalable.
The evolution of those Joint Ventures—and the drive to permanently resolve the capital constraints of Regional Markets—led directly to the current iteration of his developments: the Direct-Hold Solutions.
SOCIAL AND GOVERNANCE (ESG) — MIX-OF-USE
AGENT: The core business of the Direct-Hold Solutions is the physical execution of a standardized product: the Woodfine Buildings.
They are not building to chase macroeconomic GDP expansion; that expansion has already occurred across Regional Markets.
They are building to capture National Tenants—major law firms, accounting practices, and dental groups—who have maxed out Metro Markets and desperately require Class-A "nodes" to anchor their Distributed Economy workforce.
Without these institutional-grade physical assets, they simply cannot expand into these jurisdictions.
AGENT: To execute this, Peter engineered four distinct Woodfine Buildings under the Woodfine Professional Centres Development Class: Professional Centres, Suburban Office, Retail Select, and Tech Industrial.
Their primary mandate focuses strictly on the Professional Centres, spanning three to five Floors, and the Suburban Office, spanning six to nine Floors.
AGENT: These are not shrunk-down Metro Market towers. They engineer them from the inside out utilizing an Offset Pulled Back Core and a rigid system of Key Plans and Tiles.
This allows Woodfine to divide the Fixed Floor Plates and curate a strict Mix-of-Use typology providing for Academic, Business, Civic, Laboratory, and Medical users.
It physically engineers the space so that regardless of the layout, they offer Office Tenants nearly 100% occupational efficiency within their leaseholds.
As Woodfine dictates: "Our focus is on providing Tenants with the demising and services that allow for high efficiency within their leaseholds and the ability to build out any arrangements they may want".
AGENT: To maximize the site coverage at any specific Development Site, they deploy two backfill formats: Retail Select and Tech Industrial.
These are never stand-alone investment products; they serve strictly to complement the office component when dealing with excess land or zoning height restrictions.
Retail Select functions explicitly for Tenants such as banks, mobile phone providers, drugstores, an optometrist, barbershops, jewellery stores, vitamin stores, etc. They absolutely discourage food providers in these spaces to eliminate the parking, waste, and ventilation liabilities that degrade site efficiency.
Tech Industrial provides standard-scale buildings featuring 25% retail storefronts and 75% shop floors with grade-level panel van access, capturing the last-mile logistics and workshop operators.
AGENT: All Woodfine Buildings placed on a single Development Site must conform to a congruent, high-end aesthetic known as their Campus Design.
If a Development Site cannot accommodate their Prototype combinations effortlessly, the land does not qualify for purchase, and they bypass it entirely.
They do not redesign their buildings to fit the dirt.

ENVIRONMENTAL, SOCIAL AND GOVERNANCE (ESG) — WOODFINE BUILDINGS
AGENT: Historically, Office Tenants seeking space at Power Centres were forced to lease commercial retail bays.
The geometry of these long, rectangular spaces was fundamentally suboptimal for their Use Case.
This failure brought about “Buildings as a Retailer” concept. They treat the office component itself as a National Retailer executing a Rollout Program across the top 10% of Power Centres throughout North America and Europe.
AGENT: Traditional architects design office buildings from the outside in, prioritizing exterior aesthetics over economic utility.
They engineer Woodfine Buildings in reverse. They utilize a strict system of Key Plans and Tiles mapped to the exact furniture layouts of their target Office Tenants: Academic, Business, Civic, Laboratory, and Medical users.
This methodology defines their Fixed Floor Plates.
AGENT: They entirely reject the traditional center core design, which forces smaller tenants to navigate jagged, inefficient hallways.
Instead, they deploy an Offset Pulled Back Core. This configuration provides a continuous Corridor with a direct line of sight to the Elevator Lobbies, ensuring security and delivering nearly 100% occupational efficiency within the leaseholds.
AGENT: They do not chase abstract Environmental, Social, Governance (ESG) buzzwords.
They enforce the Geometry of Sustainability by adhering to strict, established regulations.
By hardcoding the European Lighting Directive, Swiss Air Exchange Requirements, and German Circulation Requirements into their Prototype, they create a superior Building Experience.
They supply a highly functional, formal workspace because the informal environment is already provided by the home.
AGENT: Unchecked Operating Costs are destroying tenant profitability across the broader market.
To maintain pricing power, Woodfine strictly limits their internal amenities to a Lobby Atrium, Tenant Lounge, Mail Room, Building Manager, and their Coffee/Bread concept.
Coffee/Bread is not a full-service food provider; it is an extension of the professional environment that avoids the severe parking, waste, and ventilation liabilities that degrade site efficiency.
AGENT: They do not sell abstract sustainability to the market;
they engineer measurable Environmental Performance and Human-Centric Wellness to drive asset value.
By applying a Geometry of Sustainability—anchoring their Floor Plates to the rigid standards of the European Lighting Directive and German Circulation Requirements—they physically force building efficiency.
The direct, operational outcome of this compliance is a mathematically demonstrable 30% reduction in Operating Costs for their Office Tenants.
AGENT: Legacy real estate is facing rapid obsolescence. Peter suggests office markets are separating into “investable” and “repurpose candidate”, with capital flows highly constrained for the latter.
To capture the National Tenants abandoning this legacy inventory, they secure Dual-Mandate Building Certifications.
By delivering verifiable Environmental Performance, utilizing standards like BREEAM, alongside Human-Centric Wellness certifications like WELL, they provide the exact institutional-grade infrastructure required for their regional expansion.
AGENT: Finally, by intentionally under-building the total net leasable square footage relative to the market demand, they adhere to the realities of the Circular Economy.
This deliberate short supply ensures fully leased buildings, generates waiting lists, and locks in premium pricing power across the portfolio.
GOVERNANCE (ESG) — NARROW BANKING
AGENT: The Financial Model of the Direct-Hold Solution fundamentally rejects the mechanics of traditional Private Equity.
Instead of utilizing heavy debt and selling encumbered assets to generate earnings, they are executing a model of perpetual equity.
They pay cash upfront to build unencumbered cash flows.
AGENT: To execute this, Peter engineered the Direct-Hold Solution to function as a narrow bank.
These Direct-Hold Solutions finance the procurement and development of Qualified Investments through the issuance of public, non-traded Investment Units and exchange-traded First Secured Mortgage Debentures.
AGENT: Their structural alignment with Investors is absolute. Woodfine operates on a 90-10 split.
Investors hold 90%, and their Parent Company retains a 10% ownership stake.
They take the developer profit strictly as Equity-Based Compensation. Investors act as Proxy Developers capturing the developer profit margins and unleveraged yields directly.
Because Woodfine holds their Investment Units in trust until the Investors' initial capital is returned in full, their preferred return is locked into the structure.
AGENT: Woodfine strips out the traditional profit centers. There are no acquisition fees, leasing fees, or disposition fees.
They take a fixed 1% Advisory Fee anchored strictly to the Gross Funded Value and indexed to inflation, meaning it does not inflate alongside the Net Asset Value.
They also apply a 1% Issue Cost charge to recapture the origination expense.
This framework transitions their platform from Value Add speculation to a highly efficient Cost-Plus reality.
AGENT: The mechanics of the debt execution drive this efficiency. They build the initial Woodfine Buildings with zero debt.
They do not pay banks for construction financing or traditional debt compliance costs.
Only after the assets are leased and generating cash flow does a Direct-Hold Solution self-issue First Secured Mortgage Debentures on a recognized exchange.
This provides the debt with daily liquidity, entirely eliminating the drag of amortization schedules.
AGENT: To ensure extreme financial safety, their Direct-Hold Solution cannot issue these debentures unless it maintains a strict interest coverage ratio of 1.20.
Furthermore, Woodfine is mandated to allocate up to 10% of Distributable Income to continually buy back and redeem these First Secured Mortgage Debentures at par.
This organic deleveraging systematically reduces their interest expenses and automatically increases the Distributable Income available to Investors in subsequent periods.
GOVERNANCE (ESG) — LEGACY CASH FLOW
AGENT: Their Growth Strategy is defined by a cyclical funding model they refer to as the Narrow Banking fundamentally rejecting traditional Value Add real estate plays.

By purchasing directly from suppliers for multiple buildings simultaneously, they secure National Pricing across their entire supply chain.

AGENT: This Growth Strategy requires absolute structural discipline regarding the Prototype.
If a targeted Development Site cannot perfectly accommodate the Fixed Floor Plates of the Woodfine Buildings, they do not redesign the architecture;

AGENT: Furthermore, they view Parking Ratios as a critical, non-negotiable metric for retail success, much like how Costco has engineered its parking to become a core part of the customer experience.

These high Parking Ratios guarantee safe access and egress for their Office Tenants and their clients, physically hardwiring a superior Building Experience into the asset before construction even begins.
