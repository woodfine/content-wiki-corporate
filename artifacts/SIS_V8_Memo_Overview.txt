Corporate Overview   /   Descripción Corporativa
Woodfine is a specialist manager of Direct-Hold Solutions for Institutional-Grade regional real estate.
The platform focuses strictly on the procurement, development, and management of Qualified Investments for Multi-Generational Capital seeking direct real estate exposure.
These Direct-Hold Solutions operate explicitly as Regulated Reporting Entities, ensuring absolute transparency, public audit standards, and structural integrity across all targeted jurisdictions.
Freely Transferable Investment Units  /   Unidades de Inversión de Libre Transmisión
By operating as Regulated Reporting Entities, these Direct-Hold Solutions are subject to continuous Public Oversight and mandatory Ongoing Reporting Requirements.
This rigorous regulatory standard eliminates traditional Private Equity hold periods, ensuring that all Investment Units are Freely Transferable.
Investors are never restricted by statutory lock-ups or redemption queues.
These allocations are legally permitted for private transactions, institutional transfers, or estate planning, allowing Investors to maintain absolute Custodial Control without reliance on manager-initiated exit mechanics.
Jurisdictional Framework & Flow-Through Execution  /  Marco Jurisdiccional y Ejecución de Flujo Directo
Executing this mandate relies on established, domestic jurisdictional frameworks rather than complex foreign structuring.
By applying our Universal Governing Bylaws to these standard vehicles, Woodfine engineers Self-Similar Governance across all borders.
This guarantees each vehicle delivers 100% flow-through taxation, Freely Transferable equity, and exchange-traded First Secured Mortgage Debentures on a build-and-hold asset.
To secure the narrow bank advantages across borders, the Direct-Hold Solutions utilize the optimal flow-through vehicles mandated by each sovereign jurisdiction.

Capital Framework & Domestic Alignment  /  Marco de Capital y Alineación Nacional
The platform is built upon a strict separation of debt and equity.
Public debt is issued on the exchange to absorb mass-market demand, while equity is strictly reserved for Qualified Private Capital.
To protect Investors from expensive foreign tax and compliance burdens, the Direct-Hold Solutions mandate domestic Investor alignment, restricting direct ownership to residents of the vehicle’s domicile.
This eliminates cross-border regulatory drag and ensures a clean holding.
Because the vehicle is free from the constraints of registered retirement accounts, Investors retain ultimate Custodial Control, with allocations eligible to be held in standard brokerage accounts.

Principal Alignment & Preserved Fees  /  Alineación de Principales y Tarifas Preservadas
Direct-Hold Solutions restructure the classic 2/20 model by having Woodfine take compensation as Equity-Based Compensation.
This discipline enforces a strict 1:1 alignment with Investors, with physical allocations held in trust until initial capital is fully returned.
Because developer profit is not extracted as cash, it becomes Retained Equity on the investment vehicle's balance sheet, acting as a structural reserve.
This allows for variable management fees to be replaced with a single fixed annual fee based on the Gross Funded Value, resulting in preserved fees that remain fully available to compensate the independent Asset Managers orchestrating the external Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs).
The Narrow Bank Financial Model /  El Modelo Financiero Narrow Bank
This model of perpetual equity allows the construction of the initial buildings—yielding unlevered square feet—with no requirement for pre-leasing or construction loans.
Once cash flow is stabilized, the investment vehicle issues its own First Secured Mortgage Debentures for future development.
These debentures are 100% collateralized directly by the physical Woodfine Buildings. Consequently, the Direct-Hold Solutions carry no abstract corporate debt;
all liabilities are strictly ring-fenced to the real estate. In the event of a foreclosure, the transfer of the underlying asset extinguishes the debt entirely, guaranteeing that Investors will never face capital calls to cover borrowing liabilities.
During this Leverage Cycle, the debt-to-asset value reaches no more than ~63%.
As the portfolio enters the Management Phase, a mandated 10% of Distributable Income is utilized to systematically redeem this debt.
The loan-to-value ratio continuously decreases over time, driven by organic deleveraging rather than relying on external refinancing activities.
This debt execution capability relies on a stabilized lending environment;
"consensus forecasts suggest that rates should remain relatively stable in 2026, with the Bank of Canada's policy rate held at 2.25%" (Canadian Cap Rate & Capital Markets Report, 2025).
This aligns perfectly with institutional capital that mandates "invest in 'boring,' stable, long-term infrastructure assets... [and] emphasize capital preservation and high cash yields over high leverage" (Gopinath, 2026).
The ultimate mathematical result of this compounding engine is the development of over 40% more square feet on the exact same initial capital as compared to legacy Joint Ventures.
