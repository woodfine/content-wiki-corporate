Structural Ownership.

Real estate infrastructure has a critical vulnerability: we are forcing physical buildings and corporate records into rented cloud databases. Operators are losing structural control over their own data.

To eliminate this dependency, Woodfine Management Corp. has introduced PointSav Digital Systems™, a new digital infrastructure subsidiary of Woodfine Capital Projects Inc., and initiated the deployment of a "Digital First" Operating System for real estate infrastructure.

PointSav is not a SaaS platform. It is a 3-Track operating system—comprising the PointSav Private Network™, Totebox Orchestration™, and PointSav Workplace OS™—engineered to replace rented cloud software with direct ownership and cryptographic security:

Private Network: A secure, off-internet routing layer for physical buildings and isolated data vaults.
Orchestration: Legally segregated containers for Personnel, Corporate, and Property records—designed to create a structurally verifiable "Digital Twin" of the enterprise.
Workplace OS: A secure desktop environment architected to output machine-readable files, not hidden database entries.

PointSav is built to guarantee operational trust (SOC 3) and data ownership (DARP) by hardcoding compliance into the microkernel via Machine-Based Authorization.

It is infrastructure, not overhead.
