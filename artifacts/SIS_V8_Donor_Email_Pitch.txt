Direct-Hold Solutions: A Narrow Banking Model for Real Estate Infrastructure

Woodfine Direct-Hold Solutions restructure the legacy 2/20 model. Woodfine takes Equity-Based Compensation and replaces variable management fees with a single fixed annual fee based on the Gross Funded Value. This structure preserves traditional Cash Management Fees to compensate independent Asset Managers.

Because these vehicles operate as Regulated Reporting Entities, the Investment Units are Freely Transferable. Furthermore, the First Secured Mortgage Debentures they issue are Exchange-Traded.

This architecture engineers a replicable financial model designed to fund the construction of Institutional-Grade regional real estate. Executing our Co-Location Blueprint, our Qualified Investment mandates the construction of Woodfine Buildings strictly on or adjacent to existing Power Centres. This proximity provides essential retail exposure to maximize staff retention and client accessibility.

This model utilizes Retained Equity to fund the construction of initial buildings with zero requirement for pre-leasing or construction loans. Once cash flow stabilizes, the vehicle issues First Secured Mortgage Debentures to fund subsequent development. The system strictly prohibits debt issuance unless the portfolio maintains a 1.2 Interest Coverage Ratio, funding up to 100% of Development Costs.

The mathematical result of this Development Cycle is the deployment of a synchronized portfolio of Woodfine Buildings across the United States, Canada, Spain, and Mexico.
