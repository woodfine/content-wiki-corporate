# SIS_V7_Themes: Corporate Governance & Finance

## [Theme_A] GROWTH STRATEGY (The "Direct-Hold" Thesis)
* **Target Mapping:** Maps to [H-1], [H-11]
* **Sub-Themes:**
    * **[A1] Legal Chassis:** Execution of LP, SOCIMI, and FIBRA structures.
    * **[A2] Velocity of Capital:** 100% flow-through taxation mechanics.
**Diagnostic Check:** Does the text explain that the Direct-Hold Solution requires a specific legal chassis to maintain capital velocity? Anchor the term Growth to the Rollout Program and the construction velocity mirroring National Retailers.

## [Theme_B] ESG — DIRECT-HOLD SOLUTIONS (Structural)
* **Target Mapping:** Maps to [H-9], [H-10], [H-11]
* **Sub-Themes:**
    * **[B1] Social Contract (Alignment):** Developer takes Equity-Based Compensation; 1:1 alignment.
    * **[B2] Governance (Transparency):** Strict audit and Regulated Reporting Entity standards.
**Diagnostic Check:** Does the text explain the Social Contract and the structural alignment of the Developer?

## [Theme_E] INCOME SOLUTION (The Compounding Engine)
* **Target Mapping:** Maps to [H-11]
* **Sub-Themes:**
    * **[E1] Structural Reserve:** Retained Equity functioning during the Leverage Cycle.
    * **[E2] Organic Deleveraging:** 10% of income paying down debt during the Management Phase.
**Diagnostic Check:** Does the text explain the literal mathematics of safety and debt reduction? Anchor Income to Legacy Cash Flow and Organic Deleveraging. Note: The H-11 conclusion must strictly wrap up the operational loop; introducing new product classes (e.g., Vertical Warehouses, Parking Structures) is strictly forbidden.

## [Theme_G] CAPITAL FRAMEWORK (The Diversification Gateway)
