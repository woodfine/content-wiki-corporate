### HISTORY

The Growth Strategy implemented is based on Peter's 40-year track record as a developer. It is engineered to address a financing shortfall.
Peter's background began in construction, which led to leasing to secure construction contracts. This progressed into executing design-build projects for National Retailers. He managed the lifecycle: site selection, permitting, supervision, and construction services through to turnover.
Early on, operations focused on Regional Markets. These regions possessed disposable income levels on par with Metro Markets. Peter located National Retailers throughout these Regional Markets for four decades.
This success highlighted a structural gap. Even with a National Retailer committed to a project, securing construction and take-out financing for real estate developments in Regional Markets remains difficult due to capital constraints. The commercial banking system does not adequately service these regions' construction needs.
To overcome this, Peter historically utilized a Joint Venture structure. This required financial partners capable of providing a larger percentage of equity than conventional financing dictates, along with the balance sheet capacity to guarantee conventional construction loans and take-out financing.
The legacy Joint Venture model was effective, but not scalable. The evolution of those Joint Ventures, and the objective to resolve the capital constraints of Regional Markets, led to the current iteration: the Direct-Hold Solutions.

### Product Design

The core business of the Direct-Hold Solutions is the physical execution of a standardized product: the Woodfine Buildings. Construction does not aim to chase macroeconomic GDP expansion; expansion has occurred across Regional Markets. Construction aims to capture National Tenants (law firms, accounting practices, and dental groups) who have reached capacity in Metro Markets and require Class-A "nodes" to anchor their Distributed Economy workforce. Without institutional-grade physical assets, expansion into these jurisdictions is not possible.
To execute this, Peter engineered four distinct Woodfine Buildings under the Woodfine Professional Centres Development Class: Professional Centres, Suburban Office, Retail Select, and Tech Industrial. The primary mandate focuses on Professional Centres, spanning three to five Floors, and Suburban Office, spanning six to nine Floors.
These are not reduced Metro Market towers. They are engineered from the inside out utilizing an Offset Pulled Back Core and a system of Key Plans and Tiles. This allows Woodfine to divide the Fixed Floor Plates and curate a Mix-of-Use typology for Academic, Business, Civic, Laboratory, and Medical users. The space is engineered so that Office Tenants achieve occupational efficiency within their leaseholds, regardless of layout. As Woodfine states: "Our focus is on providing Tenants with the demising and services that allow for high efficiency within their leaseholds and the ability to build out any arrangements they may want".
To maximize site coverage at a Development Site, two backfill formats are deployed: Retail Select and Tech Industrial. These are not stand-alone investment products; they complement the office component when dealing with excess land or zoning height restrictions. Retail Select functions for Tenants such as banks, mobile phone providers, drugstores, optometrists, barbershops, jewelry stores, vitamin stores. Food providers are not included in these spaces to eliminate parking, waste, and ventilation liabilities that reduce site efficiency. Tech Industrial provides standard-scale buildings featuring 25% retail storefronts and 75% shop floors with grade-level panel van access, accommodating last-mile logistics and workshop operators.
All Woodfine Buildings on a single Development Site conform to a Campus Design. If a Development Site does not accommodate Prototype combinations, the land does not qualify for purchase. Buildings are not redesigned to fit a site.

### Market Strategy

The Growth Strategy is anchored to the established Rollout Programs and construction velocity of National Retailers. Development Sites are co-located on or adjacent to existing Power Centres. This is a criterion for Qualified Investments.
Experience optioning large land parcels showed that National Retailers like Walmart, Costco, and Home Depot operate with industrial efficiency. They deploy a Prototype across locations. Walmart recently confirmed an initiative to build or remodel 800 locations, indicating the continuous scale of these operations. Woodfine mimics this execution. If the Prototype does not fit a Development Site, capital is not used to redesign the building; the next location is pursued.
Focus is exclusively on Regional Markets because economic fundamentals are strong. Professionals in these markets earn salaries comparable to Metro Markets but benefit from lower housing costs, resulting in high disposable income. Historically, Office Tenants operated out of outdated second-story units above Main Street retail.
Injecting office components directly into these retail hubs implements a Mix-of-Use strategy that transforms areas into an economic engine. National Retailers generate peak traffic outside of standard business hours. The Woodfine Buildings supply 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. occupancy, introducing a workforce that integrates with existing retail operations. This provides a new Use Case for customers familiar with the Power Centre, engineering a localized Distributed Economy.
Executing Development Sites in Regional Markets allows Woodfine to build direct, long-term relationships with municipal planners and civic authorities. This approach ensures predictable entitlement timelines and integrates physical assets into the community.

### Building Standards

Historically, Office Tenants seeking space at Power Centres leased commercial retail bays. The geometry of these spaces was suboptimal for their Use Case. This led to the “Building as a Retailer” concept. The office component is treated as a National Retailer executing a Rollout Program across Power Centres throughout North America and Europe.
Traditional architects design office buildings from the outside in, prioritizing exterior aesthetics over economic utility. Woodfine Buildings are engineered in reverse. A system of Key Plans and Tiles is utilized, mapped to the furniture layouts of target Office Tenants: Academic, Business, Civic, Laboratory, and Medical users. This methodology defines Fixed Floor Plates.
The traditional center core design is not utilized. An Offset Pulled Back Core is deployed. This configuration provides a continuous Corridor with a direct line of sight to the Elevator Lobbies, ensuring security and delivering occupational efficiency within the leaseholds.
Environmental, Social, Governance (ESG) terms are not pursued. The Geometry of Sustainability is enforced by adhering to established regulations. By integrating the European Lighting Directive, Swiss Air Exchange Requirements, and German Circulation Requirements into the Prototype, a Building Experience is created. A functional, formal workspace is supplied because the informal environment is provided by the home.
Operating Costs impact tenant profitability. To maintain pricing power, Woodfine limits internal amenities to a Lobby Atrium, Tenant Lounge, Mail Room, Building Manager, and a Coffee/Bread concept. Coffee/Bread is not a full-service food provider; it is an extension of the professional environment that avoids parking, waste, and ventilation liabilities that reduce site efficiency.
Measurable Environmental Performance and Human-Centric Wellness are engineered to drive asset value. By applying a Geometry of Sustainability (anchoring Floor Plates to the European Lighting Directive and German Circulation Requirements standards), building efficiency is achieved. The operational outcome of this compliance is a 30% reduction in Operating Costs for Office Tenants.
Legacy real estate faces obsolescence. Reports suggest office markets are separating into “investable” and “repurpose candidate,” with capital flows constrained for the latter. To capture National Tenants abandoning this legacy inventory, Dual-Mandate Building Certifications are secured. By delivering verifiable Environmental Performance (using standards like BREEAM) alongside Human-Centric Wellness certifications (like WELL), institutional-grade infrastructure required for regional expansion is provided.
By intentionally under-building total net leasable square footage relative to market demand, adherence to the Circular Economy is maintained. This supply level ensures fully leased buildings, generates waiting lists, and maintains pricing power across the portfolio.

### Financial Model

The Financial Model of the Direct-Hold Solution does not align with traditional Private Equity. Instead of utilizing debt and selling encumbered assets to generate earnings, a model of perpetual equity is executed. Cash is paid upfront to build unencumbered cash flows.
To execute this, Peter engineered the Direct-Hold Solution to function as a narrow bank. Direct-Hold Solutions finance the procurement and development of Qualified Investments through the issuance of public, non-traded Investment Units and exchange-traded First Secured Mortgage Debentures.
Structural alignment with Investors is implemented. Woodfine operates on a 90-10 split. Investors hold 90%, and Woodfine Capital Projects retains a 10% ownership stake. Developer profit is taken as Equity-Based Compensation. Investors act as Proxy Developers capturing developer profit margins and unleveraged yields directly. Because Woodfine holds Investment Units in trust until Investors' initial capital is returned in full, a preferred return is maintained.
Woodfine does not include traditional profit centers. There are no acquisition fees, leasing fees, or disposition fees. A fixed 1% Advisory Fee is applied, anchored to the Gross Funded Value and indexed to inflation, meaning it does not inflate alongside the Net Asset Value. A 1% Issue Cost charge is also applied to recapture the origination expense. This framework transitions the platform from Value Add speculation to a Cost-Plus reality.
The mechanics of debt execution drive efficiency. Initial Woodfine Buildings are constructed with zero debt. Banks are not paid for construction financing or debt compliance costs. Only after assets are leased and generating cash flow does a Direct-Hold Solution self-issue First Secured Mortgage Debentures on a recognized exchange. This provides debt with daily liquidity, eliminating amortization schedule drag.
To ensure financial safety, the Direct-Hold Solution cannot issue these debentures unless it maintains an interest coverage ratio of 1.20. Woodfine is mandated to allocate up to 10% of Distributable Income to buy back and redeem First Secured Mortgage Debentures at par. This Organic Deleveraging systematically reduces interest expenses and increases Distributable Income available to Investors in subsequent periods.

### Compounding Engine

The Growth Strategy is defined by a cyclical funding model referred to as Narrow Banking, which does not align with traditional Value Add real estate plays. Growth is engineered through the repetitive execution of the Rollout Program and the systematic issuance of debt. This perpetual equity model differs from permanent capital solutions offered by KKR, Brookfield, and Carlyle.
Operational mechanics are straightforward. Initial capital, such as the $250 million target for Canada, is raised to construct the first set of Woodfine Buildings with zero debt. Once these initial assets are fully leased and generating stable cash flow, the Direct-Hold Solutions self-issue First Secured Mortgage Debentures on a recognized exchange. The partnership is authorized to borrow up to 100% of the Development Costs, provided an interest coverage ratio of at least 1.20 is achieved immediately after borrowing. Proceeds from these debentures are then deployed to fund the procurement and construction of the subsequent set of buildings.
To maximize the efficiency of the Rollout Program, a centralized Buying Program is operated. By purchasing directly from suppliers for multiple buildings simultaneously, National Pricing is secured across the supply chain. All rebates and volume discounts are returned directly to the Direct-Hold Solutions, rather than being captured as profit by external trades and contractors.
This Growth Strategy requires structural discipline regarding the Prototype. If a targeted Development Site does not accommodate the Fixed Floor Plates of the Woodfine Buildings, the architecture is not redesigned; the location is bypassed for the next Development Site. This eliminates professional fees associated with bespoke architectural, mechanical, and electrical redesigns.
Parking Ratios are viewed as a critical metric for retail success, similar to Costco's parking design. The Governance Structure mandates a minimum 3:1000 ratio for office components, with a target of 4:1000. These Parking Ratios ensure access and egress for Office Tenants and their clients, integrating a Building Experience into the asset before construction.

When Direct-Hold Solutions are structured, the traditional corporate model is bypassed. A corporation is an entity managed by a board whose primary fiduciary duty is to the company itself, exposing profits to double taxation. By utilizing a Direct-Hold Solution, a flow-through vehicle is executed. The General Partner or Administrator owes a direct fiduciary duty to both the vehicle and the Investors. ### Capital Sourcing

Focus is not on Strategic Partnerships with institutional investors. Execution relies on a Tripartite Management Structure, which is described as a "three-legged stool".
First, Peter manages the development and construction of real estate. Second, Mathew manages compliance mandates required to maintain Regulated Reporting Entity status. Third, partnership is established with a Banking Syndicate of independent Asset Managers.
These Asset Managers serve as the entry point for Qualified Private Capital. Once Know Your Client (KYC) protocols are established, investors open accounts directly with these managers, ensuring standardized monthly statements and Custodial Control.
Building this syndicate as a multi-national group is critical because the majority of capital is anticipated to originate outside of Canada. To accommodate Multi-Generational Capital globally, Jurisdictional Diversification is engineered. Club Deal and Private SPV templates, complete with a baseline tax opinion, are provided for non-resident Asset Managers and Investors to execute direct cross-border investing.
The Direct-Hold Solutions report directly to these Club Deals and Private SPVs. This architecture allows foreign capital to optimize its tax treaties without injecting cross-border regulatory drag into the core Direct-Hold Solutions.
Because Woodfine is structurally aligned with the Investors, taking profit as Equity-Based Compensation, cash is not stripped from the vehicles. As a result, traditional management fees are Preserved Fees. These fees remain available to compensate independent Asset Managers orchestrating external Club Deals and Private SPVs, fostering a long-term alliance.
