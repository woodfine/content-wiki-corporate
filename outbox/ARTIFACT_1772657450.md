Multi-generational capital requires financial architecture engineered for transparency and direct control.

Legacy Joint Ventures present structural limitations in scaling institutional real estate development. These models limit unlevered square footage and can constrain capital liquidity.

Woodfine Management Corp. introduces Direct-Hold Solutions, a financial architecture for capital deployment. This framework is engineered to address limitations of traditional models through a Narrow Bank financial model.

Direct-Hold Solutions finance the procurement and development of Qualified Investments. This occurs through public, non-traded Investment Units and exchange-traded First Secured Mortgage Debentures.

*   Direct-Hold Solutions are engineered to develop over 40% more unlevered square feet compared to legacy Joint Ventures using the same initial capital.
*   The Narrow Bank financial model applies perpetual equity to construct assets with zero initial debt, generating unencumbered cash flow before debenture issuance.
*   Investment Units operate under Regulated Reporting Entity status, ensuring continuous public oversight and immediate transferability for investors.

This architecture secures capital preservation and long-term yield for multi-generational capital within institutional real estate.
