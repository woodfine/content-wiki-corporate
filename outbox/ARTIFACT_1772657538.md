Woodfine Capital Projects Announces Direct-Hold Solutions Real Estate Architecture

Not for distribution, Vancouver, British Columbia, updated March 2, 2026

WOODFINE MANAGEMENT CORP. ("Woodfine") has introduced the Direct-Hold Solutions real estate architecture as a system under Woodfine Capital Projects Inc. (the "Promoter"). This architecture serves as the operational framework for Woodfine and its managed investment vehicles.

The Direct-Hold Solutions are engineered to provide a framework for real estate development and management. This architecture provides for flow-through taxation, a co-location blueprint alongside National Retailers, and the issuance of Exchange-Traded First Secured Mortgage Debentures.

**The Direct-Hold Solutions Framework**
The Direct-Hold Solutions framework is engineered to support the procurement and development of Qualified Investments. This is executed through the issuance of public, non-traded Investment Units and Exchange-Traded First Secured Mortgage Debentures. This financial model is designed to support the development of Woodfine Buildings.

**Flow-Through Taxation**
Direct-Hold Solutions are structured to operate as flow-through vehicles in their respective jurisdictions. This design passes income and losses directly to Investors, applying taxation at their individual rates rather than at the corporate level. For example, vehicles like a Fideicomiso de Infraestructura en Bienes Raíces (FIBRA) in Mexico are fiscally transparent, incurring no income tax on rental income or capital gains at the trust level. This structure supports 0% corporate tax rates where applicable, with rules requiring properties to be held for leasing for a minimum period. The platform utilizes optimal flow-through vehicles mandated by each national jurisdiction to achieve these tax advantages across borders.

**Co-Location Blueprint Alongside National Retailers**
The Growth Strategy for Direct-Hold Solutions is anchored to the Rollout Programs of National Retailers. Development Sites are co-located on or adjacent to existing Power Centres. This approach aligns with the demographic research of major National Retailers such as Walmart, Costco, and Home Depot, which have established co-location strategies within specified distances of each other. This strategic positioning of Institutional-Grade Woodfine Buildings near Power Centres transposes the resilience of retail activity onto professional office spaces. This is referred to as the Institutional Retail Halo. The Co-Location Blueprint is engineered to provide professional tenants with access where their staff and clients already reside and conduct commerce.

**Issuance of Exchange-Traded First Secured Mortgage Debentures**
Direct-Hold Solutions are designed to self-issue Exchange-Traded First Secured Mortgage Debentures. This process occurs after assets are leased and generating cash flow. This provides the debt with daily liquidity. The issuance of these debentures is conditional upon maintaining an interest coverage ratio of at least 1.20. Woodfine is mandated to allocate up to 10% of Distributable Income for the systematic buy-back and redemption of these debentures at par. This Organic Deleveraging mechanism reduces interest expenses and increases Distributable Income in subsequent periods. These debentures are 100% collateralized directly by the physical Woodfine Buildings, separating corporate debt from real estate liabilities.

**Structural Governance and Investor Protections**
Direct-Hold Solutions operate as Regulated Reporting Entities, subject to continuous Public Oversight, mandatory ongoing reporting requirements, quarterly financials, and annual audits. This regulatory standard provides for Freely Transferable Investment Units, which can be used for private transactions, transfers, or estate planning without reliance on manager-initiated exit mechanics. The platform is built on a separation of debt and equity; public debt is issued on exchanges, while equity is reserved for Qualified Private Capital. The framework includes protective clauses embedded into Universal Governing Bylaws, establishing Self-Similar Governance across all jurisdictions. These bylaws prohibit capitalized interest and provide that ownership allocations function as Freely Transferable, pledgeable assets on Investor balance sheets, with oversight from Independent Oversight Committees.

For international Multi-Generational Capital, exposure is provided through Jurisdictional Diversification. External Club Deals or Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) are established in the domicile of the Direct-Hold Solution, allowing foreign Investors to tailor their individual national tax treaties. This structure is designed to integrate foreign capital without transferring international compliance burdens onto the core Direct-Hold Solutions.
